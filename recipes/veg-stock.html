Basic Vegetable Stock

A light, flavorful liquid made by simmering a combination of vegetables, herbs, and water. Common ingredients include onions, carrots, celery, garlic, and bay leaves, gently cooked to extract their essence without overpowering the stock. This clear, aromatic base is perfect for soups, stews, risottos, and sauces, offering a vegetarian-friendly alternative to meat-based stocks.

Ingredients

  • 2 large carrots
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 sprigs fresh parsley
  • 6 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • i teaspoons salt
  • 2 quarts water
