Curry Udon Noodles

Curry upon noodles is a warm, flavorful fusion dish that pairs spiced curry with soft, comforting noodles. The rich, aromatic curry—often made with spices like turmeric, cumin, and sometimes coconut milk—coats the noodles, making each bite burst with taste. Whether vegetarian or with meat, it’s a hearty, cozy meal that blends the essence of South Asian curries with the texture of East Asian noodles

Ingredients

  • 3 carrots, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 small onion, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons red curry powder
  • 5 cups hot vegetable stock
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • 8 ounces udon noodles, or more to taste
